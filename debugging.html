>> DEBUGGING <<
A code debugging event is like a problem-solving marathon for developers,involving identification and resolving errors in the code. The process involves in enhancing the participant's troubleshooting skills and improving code quality.
>> RULES <<
1.Everyone must create HackerRank accounts before the competition.
2.The participants should login with their HackerRank accounts to the link that we will provide on the day of the competition.
3.Copy and paste will be disabled in the competition.
4.Tab switching is completely restricted, if you do so you are eliminated automatically.
5.The participants will receive the entire rules and process of the competition through the email you provided while registering the day before the event.

>> Appraisal criteria <<
Total there are two rounds in the competition.

First round total 100 % of participants are going to be participated.

After first round 50 % of participants is going to eliminated from the competition based on the live leaderboard.

Second round is the final round and winners are announced after the second round based on Leaderboard.

Time management is very key because if there are two persons A and B both completed all the challenges but A do it earlier than B so the A will be in the best position in the leaderboard when compared to B.
